About Me

I am a fullstack developer with an interest in AI/ML. I still have a lot to learn which is why I am always interested in new technologies.

Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by the power of technology to transform ideas into reality. This passion led me to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a Minor in Business at Arizona State University, where I’ve maintained a perfect GPA of 4.0/4.0 and earned spots on the Dean’s List for Fall 2023 and Spring 2024.

I have recently gotten into fashion, a few of my fashion icons are @reneerapp, @chael.dot, @diyadabash. I have been actively researching queer fashion and its history.

During my academic journey, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several impactful projects and internships. At Jindal Steel and Power Ltd, I developed a fullstack application using AngularJS, Spring Boot, and SQL that increased data retrieval speed by 15%. I also led a project on dispatch optimization and finished goods forecasting, utilizing machine learning tools like PyTorch and Sklearn to enhance logistics efficiency and accuracy.

I have been drawing since I was 6, in the past I have gotten a lot into digital art. I have an instagram page @justtryingtodraw.png where I post my arts and desgins.

My internship at Ripik.ai allowed me to dive deeper into machine learning and data analytics. There, I developed algorithms for slab sizing using computer vision techniques with Ultralytics, which reduced manual labor and improved measurement accuracy by 25%.

One of my most rewarding experiences was volunteering with Teach for India, where I helped underprivileged students improve their grades by an average of 20%. This experience, along with organizing food drives and leading cybersecurity information sessions at Carmel Junior College, has fueled my passion for using technology to make a positive impact.

I love going hiking, I have climbed the nearby mountains a few times since I moved to college. I also love going swimming and playing badminton.

In my roles as Outreach Officer for oSTEM @ ASU and Outreach Chair for Qmunity at ASU, I’ve developed and implemented campus outreach programs and coordinated student activities to promote awareness of university programs and services. Managing social media content for these organizations boosted engagement by 35%.

I am always eager to learn and grow, driven by a love for creating visually engaging outputs that showcase the tangible impact of my work. Whether through interactive, data-driven applications or insightful visualizations, I am dedicated to making complex information accessible and compelling. Looking ahead, I am excited to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities to drive technological advancements together. Let’s connect and see how we can collaborate to create something amazing.
